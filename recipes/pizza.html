Delicious & Authentic Italian Pizza Margherita

This mouth-watering pizza recipe will have you wanting more and more! It is a thin-crust pizza pie that contains all three colors of the Italian flag: green from the fresh basil, white from the creamy mozarella cheese, and red from the smooth tomato sauce. In only 1 hour of heating in an oven and 15 minutes of preparation time, you can create 2 servings of this beautiful pizza!

Ingredients

  • 1 12 inch round of pizza dough, stretched
  • 3 tbsp of tomato sauce
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 3/4 oz of fresh mozarella
  • 4-5 basil leaves, roughly torn

Steps

  1. Place a pizza stone or tiles on the middle rack of your oven.
    • Turn the heat to the lowest setting, then let heat for minimum an hour.
  2. Put the sauce in the center of the stretched dough.
    • Use the back of a spoon to spread it evenly, stopping ~1/2 inches from the edges.
  3. Drizzle some olive oil over the pie.
    • Break the cheese into large pieces and place these gently on the sauce.
    • Scatter basil leaves over the top.
  4. Using a pizza peel, pick up the pie and slide it into the heated stone or tiles in the oven.
    • Bake until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbling for approximately 4-8 minutes.
  5. Serve your warm and delicious pizza pie!
