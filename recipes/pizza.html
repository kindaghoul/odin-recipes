Delicious & Authentic Italian Pizza Margherita

This mouth-watering pizza recipe will have you wanting more and more! It is a thin-crust pizza pie that contains all three colors of the Italian flag: green from the fresh basil, white from the creamy mozarella cheese, and red from the smooth tomato sauce. In only 1 hour of heating in an oven and 15 minutes of preparation time, you can create 2 servings of this beautiful pizza!

Ingredients

  • 1 12 inch round of pizza dough, stretched
  • 3 tbsp of tomato sauce
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 3/4 oz of fresh mozarella
  • 4-5 basil leaves, roughly torn
