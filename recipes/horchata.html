Mexican Horchata

Sweet & creamy cinnamon flavored drink.

Agua de horchata is a delicious traditional Mexican drink that can easily be made at home. It was originally created in North Africa, but has become a staple drink in Latinx cultures. The preperation time for this drink is ~15 minutes.

Ingredients to get you started.

  • 1 cup of white rice
  • 1 cup of sugar
    • 2/3 cup of sugar if desired to be less sweet.
  • 1/2 cup of chopped almonds
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 tbsp of vanilla
  • 1 can (12oz.) of evaporated milk
  • 1 1/2 cups of milk (or almond milk)
  • 1 liter of water
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Begin with soaking the rice, cinnamon, and almonds in a bowl of water over night.
    • Or at least for 5 hours so the rice may soften lightly.
  2. Strain the water from the mixture.
  3. Blend the mixture with evaporated milk.
    • Do this until a smoother mix is formed and the rice is completely ground.
  4. Strain the resulting liquid into a pitcher.
    • Add sugar, vanilla, and milk.
    • Mix well until everything is combined.
  5. Add water, then serve with ice.
  6. Enjoy your agua de horchata!
