Lemon Parmesan Pasta

Creamy & Vegetarian Parmesan Pasta

The easiest and most delicious pasta that you will ever make in your life! This recipe goes well with any pasta shape, any veggies you prefer, and even any protein. It is a light recipe but full of so much flavor made in only 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 9oz pasta of choice
  • 2 tbsp of butter
  • 1 tbsp of olive oil
  • 3-4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of dry white wine
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tbsp of fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup of grated parmesan cheese
