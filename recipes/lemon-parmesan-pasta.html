Lemon Parmesan Pasta

Creamy & Vegetarian Parmesan Pasta

The easiest and most delicious pasta that you will ever make in your life! This recipe goes well with any pasta shape, any veggies you prefer, and even any protein. It is a light recipe but full of so much flavor made in only 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 9oz pasta of choice
  • 2 tbsp of butter
  • 1 tbsp of olive oil
  • 3-4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of dry white wine
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tbsp of fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup of grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to the directions on the packaging.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat.
    • Add butter and olive oil.
    • Slightly reduce heat and stir in garlic, cook for 30 seconds.
  3. Add wine, salt, and pepper.
    • Cook for 4-5 minutes.
  4. Turn heat to low.
    • Add drained and cooked pasta to the pan, along with lemon juice and parmesan.
    • Stir until well combined.
  5. Serve your delicious pasta!
